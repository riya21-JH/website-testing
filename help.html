How Can You Help?

We have come a long way from where we started few years back. With likelihood of a million students in our system, it can be declared a success. However, our aim is to reach everyone on this planet, that is more than a billion students. That seems audacious but the goal is achievable because of the simplicity and low cost implementation of Kant process.

We are at 0.1% of our goal of reaching a billion count. We need help from everybody.

What do we need?

We do not have any mechanism for accepting financial help, so that is out. In any case, project does not need money.

  • Access: The Kant process can be approved and implemented by top people in state and national education departments. If you have a way to reach these people, let them know about us.
  • Visibility: Media exposure is another way to help. If you have access to national-level media, a little bit of advocacy will go a long way in spreading the word about the Kant process.

Together, we can make education accessible to everyone, no matter where they are in the world. Thank you for your support!
